How To Register:
1. To registersolo:
```--registersolo @yourname```
2.to register Team:
```--registerTeam TeamName @player1 @player2 @player3 @player4 @player5```

How to join Queue:
1.How to join Solo Queue:
	-Go in #solo-lobby channel
	-type the command ```--joinQ```
	-to see the Queue type the command ```--showQ```
2.How to join Team Queue:
	-Go in ##teams-lobby channel
	-type the command ```--joinQT TeamName```
